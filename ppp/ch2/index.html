Possibly Probably Perhaps (PPP) is an initiator of wearable adaptability currently based in Los Angeles/Shanghai, producing garments, textiles, and images in project base settings. This is the second project of PPP, a set of wearable beddings made in collaboration with a household textile company. By wrapping the body, the garments are to become an architecture of intimacy. The writing featured here is the PPP Project 2 manifesto. Please scroll through all 3 chapters to view the project in its entirety. All products available for purchase here.
