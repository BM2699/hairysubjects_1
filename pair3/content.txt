Don't forget, don't forget
Vivian's hair, cardboard boxes, elastics
2026

Description
I wasn't thinking of cutting my hair even though it's been like 2 years. Because I kinda like my hair long, but I had this vision one night when I couldn't sleep of a great pillar that holds the ceiling up, and that pillar also being held up by another kind of pillar that's more mundane and smaller. A pillar for a pillar.
My mom often reminisces about her hair thinning as she grows older and how her hair used to be so thick and strong as mines. Also, when you are tidying your room as a child, and you shove stuff underneath carpets and couches and books and believe it's all clean. All the times you make a sculpture without knowing it.
